Banana Pancakes

Pancakes are the epitome of breakfast comfort, bringing a sense of warmth and delight to any morning. The beauty of pancakes lies in their simplicity and versatility, making them a beloved treat for people of all ages. Imagine the aroma that fills the kitchen as pancakes sizzle on the griddle, their edges turning a perfect golden brown while the centers stay soft and fluffy. This tantalizing scent is just the beginning of the sensory experience that pancakes offer.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : starters breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

step 2

Prepare the wet ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

step 3

Combine wet and dry ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Gently stir until just combined. The batter will be lumpy, but that's okay. Do not overmix, as this can make the pancakes tough.

step 4

Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small amount of butter to the skillet and let it melt.

step 5

Cook the pancakes: For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges start to look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip the pancake and cook until the other side is golden brown, about 1-2 minutes more Serve: Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or butter.

ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

3/4 cup buttermilk (or milk with 1 tablespoon vinegar or lemon juice)

1/4 cup milk

1 large egg

2 tablespoons melted butter (plus more for cooking)

1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
